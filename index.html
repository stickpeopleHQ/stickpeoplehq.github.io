We have wrapped up Stick People.

We helped to make service more people-centred.

We started Stick People as a consultancy to help teams, organisations and communities grow their service design and digital capabilities.

Between 2015 and 2021, we did this by coaching, training and co-designing, always with an element of skills transfer.

We wrapped up Stick People when founders, Kathryn and Matt, moved on to other work.
